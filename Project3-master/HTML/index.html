Keep your loved ones organized in a new way

With numerous apps and methods of communication to keep up with, software which was meant to make life easier sometimes causes it to just become more cluttered. Family Manager aims to combine four important family-oriented features in one convenient location. It provides a family with a personalized board page that can be accessed by any member sharing a group account. Here you will find the ability to track one another’s locations, access a group calendar to stay up to date with family events, view/edit/create to-do lists, and view photos uploaded to a photos album to be easily shared and downloaded by everyone.
